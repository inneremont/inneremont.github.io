About

Jan Sandberg is a graphic designer and artist with an interdisciplinary approach to visual communication. Jan’s field of interest is poster design, typography, print and interactive media. Jan holds a master’s degree in visual communication from FHNW | HGK Basel, as well as a degree in con­temporary art curation acquired at ZHdK in 2019. At the moment Jan is based in Hanko, Finland.
