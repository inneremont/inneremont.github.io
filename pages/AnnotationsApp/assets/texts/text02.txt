The more items we use, the more graphic identity morphs as well. The idea that graphic identity is an confined entity, limited to a two dimensional surface, or not affected by what it is contained by is a misconception of visual media. Would the visuals appear different if the wooden stand instead was plastic, or perhaps solid? Do the items that are represented here somehow influence eachother? Please feel free to scroll the display and read the texts and reflect upon if they change how you experience the exhibition.
