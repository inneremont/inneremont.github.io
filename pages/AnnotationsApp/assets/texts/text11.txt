This poster originated in a chaotic art direction groupwork done in 2021. Our goal was to develop a set of fictional characters, a childrens book plus social media campaign for the 2021 Right Livelihood Award, however, the process became extremely complicated fast. To complicate matters more, members from the team were abroad without access to computers or telephones. This poster perhaps reflects more a stressed out and disjointed process, than a serious output for an actual campaign piece.
