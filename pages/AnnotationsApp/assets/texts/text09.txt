This section illustrates the process of manual screen printing. In screen printing,  layers are first isolated and later combined together together to make a final poster. Like letterpress printing, the process of screen printing involves a lot of manual labour. Screen printing is an overall slow process, atleast in comparison to digital printing. However, the process affords a sense of artesanal labor that the modern cmyk printer does not allow. Bare in mind, the colors were mixed by hand. Note also the final adjustments done on the plastic film.
