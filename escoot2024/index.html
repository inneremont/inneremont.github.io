Escoot2024

Escoot2024 started as an experiment in Blender—playing around with compositions, curves, and lighting—before merging it with a 3D model. It wasn’t meant to be anything serious, just me having some fun with Blender. When it comes to loitering e-scooters—which played a big part in sparking the idea and keeping me doodling in Blender—my issue isn’t so much with the users but with the lack of regulation and slow development of infrastructure. It feels like yet another case of letting the “invisible hand” just manage something new without properly addressing the rules and systems needed to manage it. I get the sense that many decision-makers have missed out on crucial lessons from urban planning—maybe it’s time to start an initiative to get city councils reading classic books like Jane Jacobs as a starting point? Anyway, what started as one idea eventually morphed into something else entirely, and you can see the resulting character map here.

→ Blender3D, letterforms, experiment, symbols, F4, QR Code
